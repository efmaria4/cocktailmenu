Mojito

Ingredients

  • 50ml Rum
  • 20ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 30ml Sugar Syrup
  • 2 Lime Wedges
  • 8 Mint Leaves
  • Splash of Soda Water

Instructions

  1. Add the lime wedges on the bottom of the glass
  2. Add the sugar syrup, the lime juice, the mint leaves and the rum
  3. Add ice and mix
  4. Add soda water and stir gently

Details

  • Method: Stirring
  • Glassware: Highball
  • Garnish: Lime and Mint Leaves
