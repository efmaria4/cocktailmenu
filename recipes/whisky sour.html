Whisky Sour

Ingredients

  • 50ml Bourbon/Whisky
  • 30ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 15ml Sugar Syrup
  • 3 Dashes Fee Foam/15-20ml Methyl/1tbsp Egg White

Instructions

  1. Add all the ingredients to the shaker (without adding ice).
  2. Dry shake for 10-15 seconds.
  3. Add ice to the shaker and shake for another 10-15 seconds.
  4. Double strain into a chilled glass.

Details

  • Method: Dry Skaking & Shaking
  • Glassware: Rocks
  • Garnish: Lemon Zest
