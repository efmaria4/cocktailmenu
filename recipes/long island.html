Long Island

Ingredients

  • 15ml Gin
  • 15ml Vodka
  • 15ml Tequila Blanco
  • 15ml Rum
  • 15ml Triple Sec
  • 15ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Coca-Cola to top up

Instructions

  1. Add the ingredients one by one into a glass with ice.
  2. Top up with Coca-Cola.

Details

  • Method: Building
  • Glassware: Double Old Fashioned
  • Garnish: Lemon Wedge
