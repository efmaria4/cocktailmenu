Golden Drop

Ingredients

  • 2 Dashes Angostura Bitters
  • 20ml Fresh Orange Juice
  • 20ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 2 tsp Honey
  • Sparkling Water to top up

Instructions

  1. Add the lemon juice and the orange juice to the shaker.
  2. Add the honey and the bitters to the shaker.
  3. Shake for 10-15 seconds.
  4. Strain the mixture into a glass with ice.
  5. Top with sparkling water.

Details

  • Method: Shaking & Building
  • Glassware: Rocks
  • Garnish: Lemon Wedge or/>amd Mint Leaves
