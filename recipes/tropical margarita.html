Tropical Margarita

Ingredients

  • 40ml Tequila Blanco
  • 20ml Tequila Tropical
  • 20ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 20ml Triple Sec
  • 10ml Passion Fruit Puree (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rim the glass with lime and dip into salt or sugar.
  2. Shake all the ingredients with ice.
  3. Double strain into the chilled glass.

Details

  • Method: Shaking
  • Glassware: Cocktail Glass
  • Garnish: Lime and salt rim
