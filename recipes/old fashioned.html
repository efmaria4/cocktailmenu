Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 60ml Bourbon/Whiskey
  • 4 dashes angostura bitters
  • 10ml Sugar Syrup
  • 1 Orange Wedge

Instructions

  1. Add the orange wedge to the glass and muddle it.
  2. Add the sugar syrup and bitters to the glass and stir.
  3. Add the bourbon/whiskey to the glass and stir.

Details

  • Method: Muddling and Stirring
  • Glassware: Old Fashioned
  • Garnish: Orange Zest
