Paloma

Ingredients

  • 50ml Tequila Blanco
  • 20ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • Grapefruit Soda to top up

Instructions

  1. Rim the glass with lime and dip into salt.
  2. Add the tequila and the lime juice to the glass.
  3. Fill the glass with ice and add stir.
  4. Add ice and grapefruit soda and stir gently
  5. Add more ice and garnish

Details

  • Method: Building
  • Glassware: Collins
  • Garnish: Grapefruit slice
