Cucumber Snap

Ingredients

  • 50ml Gin
  • 6-8 Slices of Cucumber
  • 20ml Lime Juice

Instructions

  1. Add the cucumber slices to the shaker and muddle them.
  2. Add the gin and the lime to the shaker and shake with ice.
  3. Slide a long cucumber ribbon into the glass so it hugs the inside.
  4. Add ice.
  5. Double strain into a chilled glass.

Details

  • Method: Muddling and Shaking
  • Glassware: Old Fashioned
  • Garnish: Cucumber roll on stick
