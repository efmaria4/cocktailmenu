Pink Thorn

Ingredients

  • 80ml Grapefruit Soda
  • 40ml Grapefruit Juice
  • 20ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • 1 sprig Rosemary

Instructions

  1. Add the grapefruit juice, the lime juice and the honey to the shaker and shake.
  2. Add an ice cube and shake for 10-15 seconds.
  3. Strain the mixture into a glass with ice.
  4. Top with grapefruit soda.

Details

  • Method: Shaking & Building
  • Glassware: Rocks
  • Garnish: Grapefruit slice and sprig of rosemary
