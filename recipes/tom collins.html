Tom Collins

Ingredients

  • 60ml Gin
  • 30ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 20ml Sygar Syrup
  • Soda to top up

Instructions

  1. Add the ingredients to the shaker and shake.
  2. Strain the mixture into a chilled glass.
  3. Top up with soda.

Details

  • Method: Shaking
  • Glassware: Collins
  • Garnish: Lemon Wedge
