Dark and Stormy

Ingredients

  • 40ml Dark Rum
  • 20ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • Ginger Beer to top up

Instructions

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Pour in the dark rum and lime juice.
  3. Top up with ginger beer.

Details

  • Method: Building
  • Glassware: Highball
  • Garnish: Lime Wedge
