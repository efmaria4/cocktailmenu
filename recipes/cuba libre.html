Cuba Libre

Ingredients

  • 50ml Rum
  • 2 Lime Wedges
  • Coca-Cola to top up

Instructions

  1. Squeeze 1 lime wedge into a highball glass and drop it in.
  2. Fill the glass with ice.
  3. Pour in the rum and stir.
  4. Top up with Coca-Cola and stir once.
  5. Squezee the second lime wedge into the glass and drop it in.

Details

  • Method: Building
  • Glassware: Collins
  • Garnish: Lemon Wedge
