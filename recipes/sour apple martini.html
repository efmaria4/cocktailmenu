Sour Apple Martini

Ingredients

  • 60ml Vodka
  • 20ml Sour Apple Puree
  • 10ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 15ml Triple Sec

Instructions

  1. Add all the ingredients to the shaker.
  2. Double strain into a chilled glass.

Details

  • Method: Shaking
  • Glassware: Cocktail
  • Garnish: Green Apple Slice
