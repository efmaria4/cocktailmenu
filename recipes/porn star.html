Porn Star

Ingredients

  • 50ml Vodka
  • 20ml Passion Fruit Puree
  • 15ml Vanilla Syrup
  • 20ml Lime Juice

Instructions

  1. Add all the ingredients to the shaker.
  2. Double strain into a chilled glass.

Details

  • Method: Shaking
  • Glassware: Cocktail Glass
  • Garnish: Half Passion fruit
