Blush Breeze

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Cubed Fresh Watermelon
  • 6-8 Mint Leaves
  • 20ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • Soda Water to top up

Instructions

  1. Muddle the watermelon and the mint leaves in the glass.
  2. Add the lime juice and the ice.
  3. Fill the glass with soda and stir gently.

Details

  • Method: Muddling & Stirring
  • Glassware: Rocks
  • Garnish: Watermelon Slice
