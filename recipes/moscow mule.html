Moscow Mule

Ingredients

  • Lime Wedges
  • 60ml Vodka
  • Ginger Beer to top up

Instructions

  1. Squeeze the lime wedges and drop in the mug
  2. Add the vodka and the soda water and stir
  3. Add soda water and stir gently

Details

  • Method: Building
  • Glassware: Copper Mug
  • Garnish: Lime Wedge
