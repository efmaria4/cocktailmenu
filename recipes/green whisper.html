Green Whisper

Ingredients

  • 5 Slices of Cucumber
  • 5 Mint/Basil Leaves
  • 20ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 2 tsp Honey
  • Soda Water to top up

Instructions

  1. Muddle the cucumber slices, the honey and the basil/mint in a spare glass.
  2. Add the soda and stir.
  3. Fill the rocks glass with ice.
  4. Strain the mixture into the rocks glass.

Details

  • Method: Muddling
  • Glassware: Rocks
  • Garnish: Basil/Mint or/and Cucumber
